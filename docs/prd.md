# NeebapNaebap MVP PRD

## Goal

Let close friends upload lunch photos and decide who won across taste, value, and healthy/management dimensions.

## MVP scope

1. Friend/group creation.
2. Image upload through presigned storage URLs.
3. Battle entry creation from uploaded images.
4. Multi-category friend voting.
5. Results by category and overall winner.
6. Community share request when friends want outside votes.

## Non-goals for first skeleton

- Payments.
- Restaurant database.
- Public-first feed.
- Heavy recommendations.
